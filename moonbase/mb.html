MOONBASE

What is Moonbase?

In the Year 2020 Earth A.D., GHOULS discovered $BASED. $BASED contains strange powers that are difficult, if not dangerous, to wield effectively. $BASED doesn't interact with other elements very well. To solve this problem, GHOULS built Moonbase and an entire ecosystem was borne. Anyone can stake $BASED in Moonbase to get its refined derivative—mbBASED—the native currency of Based Protocol. mbBASED is a standard token used in the Ethereum Galactic Computer. It works just as any other ERC20-type token and can be used as a composable asset in any Ethereum protocol.

What are Rovers?

GHOULS bootstrapped this new moon economy by installing a public Rover Launch Console. Rovers tap into the elemental form of $BASED. To get Based, partner projects allocate a portion of their own native token in a Rover. Once the Rover is launched, those tokens get vested—slowly unlocking over the course of one Earth-year. At any moment, anyone can convert the available tokens into $BASED by accessing a special function in the Rover console. By doing so, they're able to pilfer 1% of that $BASED. The other 99% gets split between everyone holding mbBASED. This is a safest and most-secure method to get $BASED. GHOULS will always be on Moonbase ready to help harness the continuous stream of memetic energy flowing from $BASED.

There are other methods to get $BASED, but they remain extremely volatile, dangerous and potentially harmful to your health.
